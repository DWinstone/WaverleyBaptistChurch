Welcome to Waverley Baptist Church


Where everyone is welcome to come and join us.

We aim to make contact with encourage others to join us in our life-changing Christian journey.

We are a friendly Christian community where we welcome others to join us in our worship and service to God.

Our vision is to make an impact for God, here in Waverley, by helping people understand the enriching messages of eternal
hope given to us by Jesus Christ through His words and deeds.

Everyone is welcome, no matter your age, beliefs, or background. Come just as you are - we'd love to get to know you better.


Jesus Christ said "I am the Way, the Truth and the life". There is Salvation in NO other name Without Love, we are nothing
